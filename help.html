This is a taggable image database.
Search for images with a space-separated list of tags: "sky mountain" (without the quotes) will search for images tagged with "sky" and "mountain". Leave the search blank or click Browse to list all images.

Searching will take you to the image list. The image list displays the 20 most popular tags. Click on an image on the image list to view it in full size and see its tags. Click on a tag to search for it if you don't feel like typing.
